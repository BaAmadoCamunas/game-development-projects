# A Flower's Dream: The Beginning

*A Flower's Dream: The Beginning* is a visual novel developed as part of my **Bachelor's Degree in Video Game Design and Development** at Universidad Rey Juan Carlos.

Players take the role of a florist who has recently opened a flower shop in a new city. Through dialogue choices, flower bouquet creation and interactions with customers, the story evolves towards friendship, romance or conflict, leading to multiple possible endings.

---

## Overview

The project combines traditional visual novel mechanics with gameplay systems such as flower bouquet creation and relationship management. Player decisions directly influence the narrative and determine one of several possible endings.

---

## Features

- Branching narrative with multiple endings
- Dialogue choices that affect character relationships
- Flower bouquet creation mini-game
- Romance and friendship progression system
- Unlockable memory gallery
- Customizable player name, avatar and gender
- Available for PC and mobile devices
